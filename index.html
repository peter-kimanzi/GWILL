About us

The Blessed Heart Community Center was founded in the year 2012 and later registered in the year 2013 in July with the vision to be a center for excellence for children in the KCC slum. The Blessed heart self- help group was started by mothers in the KCC slums in Umoja 3 next to KCC dairy factory.
